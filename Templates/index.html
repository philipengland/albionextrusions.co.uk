About Us

Established in 1986, Albion has become one of the UK's furniture industry leading suppliers. By providing an "all under one roof" range of products for the kitchen and bedroom markets we believe that our customer service, prices and quality are second to none.

Over the years we have built a reputation for products that meet the requirements of our customers and we continue to take pride in doing so.
